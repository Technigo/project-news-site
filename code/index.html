- How to cook vegetables properly

Cut your favorite vegetables into even pieces, spread them out on a baking sheet, and drizzle with olive oil. Season and roast in the oven until golden brown and tender. This method enhances the natural sweetness of the vegetables and gives them a delightful crispiness.
