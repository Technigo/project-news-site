Elon Musk buys Sweden

Business magnate Elon Musk completed a private acquisition of the country of Sweden, on January 1st, 2023. Musk began traveling to Sweden just a few years earlier and has since acquired 120 private properties and 25 local companies. When asked about the reason of acquiring the country, he simply stated “Kanelbullar”. What this will mean for the country of Sweden and its inhabitants, only time will tell.
