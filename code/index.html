BLACKPINK’s “How You Like That” Remains On Top; K-Pop Music Chart 2020

BLACKPINK’s “How You Like That” is our No. 1 song for the sixth straight week! The song is now tied with Red Velvet’s “Psycho” as the songs to top our chart for the longest period of time this year. Congratulations again to BLACKPINK!

Debuting at No. 2 is (G)I-DLE’s special summer song “DUMDi DUMDi.” Written by leader Soyeon and producer Pop Time, “DUMDi DUMDi” is a dance track with a tropical base and Moombahton rhythm.

Rounding out the top three is Hwasa’s “Maria,” moving down one spot to No. 3.

One more song newly entered the top 10. Moving up 31 spots to No. 4 is Jessi’s “NUNU NANA,” the title track from her mini album “NUNA.” One of Jessi’s biggest hits yet, “NUNU NANA” is a hip hop dance song with powerful trap and pop elements.
