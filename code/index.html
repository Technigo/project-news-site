BREAKING: A WILD CHASE IN THE HEAT!

Published 31/7/2023 16:44

She has done it again, folks. Looking at 11-year-old Odette and her daily antics, you would not guess how fast this Jack-Cairn lady can run when given the chance.

This dog doesn't particularly enjoy excercise - except when there's an element of chase tied to it", a source tells Terrier Times. On Monday the 31st of July, Odette managed to wiggle herself out of her harness during her afternoon walk.

She was chased around the neighbourhood for almost two hours before getting caught. The day was quite hot, and as Odette has quite a thick fur, she tired herself out eventually and gave the worried search party a chance to get her to safety. An adventure like this could be quite dangerous for and old dog with a thick fur. "Her heart was beating quite fast for a while there, but luckily it calmed down after a while", the owner says wth relief.
