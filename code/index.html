The Blaze

French-British rapper Octavian and French dance music duo The Blaze have teamed up to offer a new single.

French-British rapper Octavian and French dance music duo The Blaze have teamed up to offer a new single, “Somewhere.” The three-and-a-half-minute cut sees both artists entering new territory, weaving together their distinct sounds to create a complementing track that can satisfy both of their audiences.

“Somewhere” marks The Blaze’s first release since 2018’s debut album DANCEHALL. Meanwhile, Octavian followed his 2018 debut Spaceman with Endorphins, a 12-track project that featured the likes of A$AP Ferg, Smokepurpp and Skepta to name a few.
