Red Rock Canyon - a true feast for nature enthusiasts

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area in Nevada, USA is visited by more than three million people each year.

The conservation area is characterised by a dramatic landscape of large red rock formations, a set of sandstone peaks and walls which were formed by thrust faults (thrust faults occur when a section of land slips over another at a low angle).

Hiking and trails as well as other reacreational activities, such as biking, rock climbing and camping are the most popular activities at the Red Rock.
