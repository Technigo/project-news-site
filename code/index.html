Researchers say solution against dog allergies is 'within grasp'

There may be hope on the horizon for those who suffer from dog allergies, thanks to recent research efforts from Osaka Prefecture University in Japan.

Researchers looking to artificially induce immune tolerance have for the first time identified candidates for those parts of the molecules that make up dog allergens - which could give us a 'dog allergy vaccine'.
