Island hopping

It’s easy to get out to the thousands of islands in the Bohuslän archipelago with the help of local ferry and boat companies. You’ll find tips here on how to find your own favourite spot on the west coast – from the Kosterhavet sea in the north, to Gothenburg’s archipelago in the south.
A number of different boat companies operate along the coast of Bohuslän. We’ve gathered them all here with the information you need to be able to plan your trip.

Seafood safari

Outside the summer season you find the genuine Bohuslän - salty, barren, windswept and calm. Head out on a shellfish tour, to catch the finest prawns, mussels, oysters, langoustines and lobster. Add a little salt to your life.

Join a local fisher for a shellfish tour in the beautiful archipelago. You get to help prepare the langoustine and lobster pots or harvest the oysters and mussels.
