Staying for christmas?

For all of you staying with us this winter it's time to start prepping. Bulking season is here. Summer is over and with that beach season. So start to eat as much as possible. That extra fat will be useful when the cold is here and food is limited. Don’t worry about beach 2022, that extra fat will be gone by march and you will regret that you didn’t eat even more during the fall. Remember, a fat winter bird is a happy winter bird!
