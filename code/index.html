HOTTEST RELEASE NEWS

Right out of the box. Three versions of the Nike Dunk has released. Arriving with three colorways to bring a new perspective of the Dunks iconic color-blocing.

Crystals Forever

2020, November 11 - With a theme of air and sunshine Cactus Plant Flea Market has done a brilliant collaboration again. The Nike Dunk is shining bright with actual Swarovski® crystals.

Motha' Earth

2020, September 18 - Ruohan Wang (Berlin-Based illustrator) gives the Flyleather pack some optimistic colour and motion. Made with 50% recycled leather fibre.

Rip & Tear it to You

2020, April 16 - Nike Air Force 1 LX releases a unique design for you to be creative in your own way. It is what is underneath that is the intriguing part. You can peel back the upper layer to make your own colourway.

Naomi Osaka

2020, December 4 -The Nike x Naomi Osaka x CDG Blazer celebrates the irrefutable power of the tennis star’s cultural footprint. Additional details include Osaka's signature along with the handwritten phrase "Home is Where the Heart is.”

‘Para-noise’

2020, November 5 - One year ago, Seoul-born hip-hop star G-DRAGON released his first collaborative Nike Air Force 1 “Para-noise". In 2020, the Nike Air Force 1 “Para-noise” reverses its color scheme to white, but retains its self-expressive character.
