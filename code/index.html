Nike Will Design Sneakers for the Metaverse

Swoosh members will compete to create digital kicks with the brand’s design staff and bag a royalty cut. But not even Nike knows how it’s going to work.

Swoosh exists on a domain named “.nike” and will be a digital space for registered members. It’s currently in beta, and registration to join opens in May. The platform will initially focus on community building and present members with “challenges” to get a sense of what they want to see. The brand’s first virtual collection will launch in August 2023 shaped through interactive voting.
