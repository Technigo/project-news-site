Tourism is travelling for pleasure or to enjoy yourself away from the place you live. People do this for many different reasons – to have fun, visit other countries and learn about other cultures or just relax from stressful working life. Tourists go to various destinations, countries with great landmarks, places with lovely beaches or simply areas of wilderness and untouched nature. In the last few decades tourism has grown very much, mostly because people’s lifestyles have changed. Tourists go to various destinations.
