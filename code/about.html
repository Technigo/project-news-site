ABOUT

Who are the terriers anyway? Let's find out!

ODETTE
  • Name: Odette
  • Age: 11 years old
  • Breed: Jack Russell/Cairn terrier mix
  • Likes: Eating (especially Dentastix bones), sleeping, licking people's feet.
  • Dislikes: Being ignored when she is persistently asking for more treats, other dogs (except for Tuutikki), prolonged excercise.
  • Special talents: Joining the choir with her hauntingly beautiful howl when somebody else is singing, always getting her way.
TUUTIKKI
  • Name: Tuutikki
  • Age: 2 years old
  • Breed: Jack Russell terrier
  • Likes: Running around, barking, playing tug-of-war with plushies.
  • Dislikes: Car traveling
  • Special talents: Stealing food from people's plates when they aren't looking.
